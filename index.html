Our Mission

Our mission is to help educate the world about some of our favorite species of sea turtles. Unfortunately, sea turtles are continually forced to face deadly and unfair dangers throughout their lives. It is our goal to spread awareness about these dangers and provide people with resources and opportunities to join a movement to preserve these amazing creatures. Become a Turtle Trooper today!







Types of Sea Turtles

There are a large variety of different sea turtle species that live across several types of habitats. On the website, we take a thorough look at the leatherback, loggerhead, and the kemp ridley species.
