Fun Facts About The Kemp Ridley!

  • They are the smallest spieces of sea turtle with their shell only reaching around 24 to 28 inches.
  • On average, the kemp ridley will weigh around 100 pounds when they reach adulthood
  • Kemp ridleys rarely venture into deep waters. The deepest depth that a kemp may tavel is aproximately 160 feet below surface level.
  • Kemp ridley turtles are the most endangered species of sea turtle due to the low ammout of beaches that they lay their eggs at.
